Knowledge Computing
We are an interdisciplinary research lab at the
Computer Science & Engineering Department, University of Minnesota, Twin Cities
{% include section-header.html title="Our Research" %}

Our research lies at the intersection of computer science and spatial sciences. We build algorithms and intelligent systems for discovering, collecting, fusing, and analyzing data from heterogeneous sources to solve real-world problems. Recently, we focus on developing machine learning frameworks that exploit unique spatial data properties and prior structural knowledge to tackle problems in natural language processing, computer vision, and spatiotemporal data analysis. Learn more about our research. (We moved from the Spatial Sciences Institute at the University of Southern California (USC) to Minnesota in 2021. Here's our lab page at USC.)
